ENEX → OneNote (One-Click Helper)

Upload one or more exported Evernote .enex notebook files, then click Convert & Download. You will get a ZIP containing one .docx per note, ready to drag into OneNote.

How to import to OneNote (easiest path)

  1. Open OneNote desktop app (Windows/Mac).
  2. Unzip the downloaded file.
  3. Select all .docx files and drag them into the target OneNote section.
  4. OneNote creates one page per document automatically.

Tip: This preserves note text, basic formatting, tables, checkboxes, links, and inline images in most cases.
